I want to build a platform that allow underserved communities who don't have easy access to finance to tokenize their assets such as house or land and then use this as collateral in order to borrow USDC. I need a way to create the assets and ensure there is sufficient verification of the asset. I need a way to issue the asset onchain and ensure the community member get a wallet that can own the asset, but where they don't need to manage the private key, yet have access to the account. I need a lending pool where liquidity providers can place liquidity. I need a management portal for liquidity providers to manage their liquidity and get reports on performance. The platform needs to have an economic model that charges the borrower and rewards the liquidity provider. I also need reliable valuation feeds for the underlying assets. Lastly, I need a management portal where I can see performance of the platform over time.
